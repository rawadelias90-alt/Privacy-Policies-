# Privacy Policy

This GPT uses optional external actions to retrieve live travel-support information for the Bali Trip Co-Pilot.

## Actions Used

### Weather Forecast Action

This GPT may use a weather action to retrieve forecast information from Open-Meteo.

The weather action sends only the selected location coordinates, forecast days, and timezone to the weather API.

### Currency Exchange Action

This GPT may use a currency exchange action to retrieve live exchange-rate information from Frankfurter API.

The currency action sends only currency codes such as AED, IDR, USD, or EUR, and does not send personal information.

### Google Places Action

This GPT may use Google Places API through a Google Apps Script proxy to search for restaurants, beaches, hotels, cafés, attractions, and other places.

The Google Places action may send place names, area names, or user-entered search text to Google Places API. It may retrieve public place information such as ratings, reviews, opening hours, price level, photos metadata, place types, addresses, and Google Maps links.

Users should avoid entering private home addresses or sensitive personal locations unless necessary.

## Personal Information

This GPT does not intentionally collect, store, sell, or share personal information through these actions.

Users should avoid entering sensitive personal information into weather, currency, location, or travel-planning requests.

## Third-Party Services

Open-Meteo, Frankfurter API, Google Apps Script, and Google Places API may process API requests according to their own privacy policies and service terms.

Exchange rates, weather forecasts, and place information are provided for planning convenience only and may differ from banks, card issuers, airport exchange counters, hotels, weather apps, Google Maps, local venues, or service providers.
